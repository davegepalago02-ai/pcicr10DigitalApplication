### ‚ú® Key Features

*   **Offline Capability:** Fully functional without an internet connection. Data is stored locally in the browser.

    *   Automatic calculation of Insurance Cover based on crop rates.

*   **Reporting Dashboard:**
    *   Real-time tracker of monthly enrollments.
